# Risk methodology

Risk is a weighted mean of available 0–100 dimensions. Default weights are delivery 15%, quality 15%, capacity/financial/geopolitical/single-source/lead-time 10% each, and commodity/commercial/compliance/continuity 5% each. Missing inputs are excluded and explicitly reduce completeness; they are never converted to zero. Bands: Low 0–24, Moderate 25–49, High 50–69, Critical 70–100. Responses expose inputs, contributions, missing dimensions and model version.
